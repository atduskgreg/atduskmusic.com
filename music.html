All Our Music

We decided to make all of our music available for free through our website because, above everything else, we want people to listen to it.

Towards this end, we've decided to license our two older records, Heights and The Summer of Promises Kept under a Creative Commons 2.5 Attribution-NonCommercial 2.5 License. That means that you can download these songs, pass them around to your friends, make remixes, use them in your You Tube movies, etc. as long as you give us credit and don't charge any money.

Speaking of money. . .while we want as many people as possible to hear our music, we also wouldn't mind taking in enough money from it ourselves to fill our gas tanks, buy some Red Vines, and, above all be able to keep on making more music. So, if you like our songs and want there to be more of them, click on the button below. It'll take your off to Pay Pal where you can contribute as much as you like. You can also buy each of our records from CD Baby.
